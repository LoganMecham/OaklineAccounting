About Logan Mecham

Hi, I’m Logan Mecham. I run Oakline Tax and Accounting and I specialize in construction and service based businesses. I like working with people who build real things, show up early, and take pride in their work.

Background

I’ve always been hands on. I started a small landscaping business called Busy Bee Odd Jobs and learned fast what it takes to keep jobs organized, keep customers happy, and make sure the numbers actually work. I also worked with my brother’s pool company and got a close look at how service businesses run day to day, scheduling, materials, labor, and making sure each job stays profitable. My dad used to own a roofing business, so I grew up around the realities of trades and construction and the pressure that comes with running work, crews, and cash flow.

What I do and who I help

I help construction and service businesses stay on top of bookkeeping, payroll, quarterly tax planning, and year end filings. The goal is simple:

  • Clean books
  • Predictable taxes
  • Clear decisions based on real numbers

Why clients work with me

  • I get how trades and service work actually feels: seasonality, job based income, materials, labor, and tight timelines.
  • I keep things straightforward and easy to follow.
  • I communicate clearly and I do not overcomplicate the process.
  • I focus on building a long term relationship, not just a one time tax return.

Services focus

  • Bookkeeping and monthly financials
  • Payroll guidance and payroll support
  • Quarterly estimated tax planning
  • Business tax returns and personal tax returns
  • General consulting for clean systems and better decisions
